Sign Up / Membership

As a member of the Australian Digital Gig Workers Union (ADGWU), you gain access to a wide range of benefits designed to protect, empower, and support gig workers across Australia:

  • 20% discount on legal and dispute resolution services
  • Workshops and online training on client management and workplace safety
  • Great-value private health insurance via UnionHealth
  • Reliable, affordable vehicle insurance via DeliveryProtect
  • Savings and discounts on goods through UnionShopper
  • Exclusive updates via the ADGWU newsletter
  • Invitation to our annual national conference in Sydney

How to Join

To become a member, follow these steps:

  1. Fill in your contact and work details in the Membership Application form below.
  2. Our team will process your application within 2–3 business days and send you a confirmation email.
  3. Click the link in the email to make your membership confirmation payment (credit or debit card).
  4. Once payment is confirmed, your membership is active and benefits begin immediately.

Become a member and join us in the fight for fairer wages and protections for gig workers.
