Sign Up / Membership

As a member of the Australian Digital Gig Workers Union, you get access to the following benefits:

  • Access to legal and dispute resolution services at a 20% discounted rate.
  • Workshops and Online Training Resources focused around client management and workplace safety practices.
  • Great-value private health insurance via UnionHealth.
  • Reliable and cheap vehicle insurance via DeliveryProtect.
  • Savings and Discounts on a wide variety of goods via UnionShopper.
  • Exclusive updates from the ADGWU newsletter.
  • An invitation to the national ADGWU conference hosted annually in Sydney.

How to Join

In order to become a member, you must do the following:

  1. Provide your contact and work details in the Membership Application Form.
  2. Once completed, it will be processed by ADGWU staff and you will receive an email reply to finalise your application in 2–3 business days.
  3. This email contains a link to make a membership confirmation payment via credit or debit card.
  4. Once that has been completed, your membership is confirmed and you can enjoy the benefits that ADGWU has to offer.

Become a member, and join us in the fight for fairer wages and workplace protections for gig workers.
