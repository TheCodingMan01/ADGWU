Sign Up / Membership

As a member of the Australian Digital Gig Workers Union, you get access to the following benefits:

  • 20% discount on legal and dispute resolution services
  • Workshops and online training on client management and workplace safety
  • Great-value private health insurance via UnionHealth
  • Reliable, affordable vehicle insurance via DeliveryProtect
  • Savings and discounts through UnionShopper
  • Exclusive updates via the ADGWU newsletter
  • Invitation to our annual national conference in Sydney
