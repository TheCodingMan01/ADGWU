About

ADGWU was founded to protect and uplift gig workers in a rapidly evolving, digital-first workforce. From food delivery riders to content moderators or streamers, we represent the voices of the Australians that need it most.

Learn More

Our Mission

At ADGWU, our mission is to champion the rights, dignity, and prosperity of gig workers across Australia in an increasingly digital and decentralized economy. As the nature of work evolves, we stand united to ensure fair pay, secure working conditions, and equitable access to benefits for all independent contractors, freelancers, and platform-based workers.

  • Fair Representation: Giving gig workers a collective voice in policy-making, platform governance, and workplace standards.
  • Economic Security: Pushing for universal access to healthcare, superannuation, paid leave, and income protection.
  • Digital Justice: Ensuring transparency and accountability in algorithmic management and data use.
  • Community and Solidarity: Building an inclusive network of support and opportunity for all gig workers.
