Reforms Have Arrived: A Look Back in Time

Sydney · January 18, 2034

From early guardrails for drivers and delivery workers to a final push on platform transparency, this retrospective tracks how advocacy, inquiries, and organising shaped today’s protections.

In 2024, the federal government introduced first-step protections. By 2030 it was clear loopholes remained — particularly around unpaid waiting time and automated pay changes. Building on the work of TWU and AUWU, ADGWU campaigned to close those gaps and standardise rights across platforms.
