Union Report Reveals Rise in Algorithmic Wage Theft

Melbourne · September 12, 2034

Our Clocked Out by Code report documents systemic deductions and retroactive pay cuts that workers cannot contest.

  • 25% of workers reported deductions tied to unrealistic pickup times
  • Widespread retroactive wage reductions with no appeal process
  • Platforms such as Uber and RideAwAI cited frequently

“This isn’t a bug,” said analyst Sophie Ren. “It’s the business model: control without accountability.” If you’ve been affected, reach out.
