ADGWU Files Landmark Case Against UStream Pty Ltd

Sydney · June 18, 2035

Filed in the Federal Court, the class action alleges systematic contractor misclassification and algorithmic control akin to employment.

  • Quota-based performance metrics and penalties
  • Shadowbans and opaque moderation affecting reach and income
  • Automated demonetisation without a meaningful right of appeal

“Digital creators aren’t entrepreneurs when they’re scored and punished by systems they can’t control,” said Dr. Matt Phillips, lead counsel. Members can register interest for case updates via the contact form.
