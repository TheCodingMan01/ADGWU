Victory for Fair Pay: Parliament Passes the Digital Workers Rights Act

Canberra · March 3, 2033

In a historic win for ADGWU members, the Digital Workers Rights Act 2033 has passed both houses of Parliament, cementing baseline protections across the gig and creator economy.

  • Minimum hourly pay guarantees
  • Superannuation inclusion
  • Collective bargaining rights
  • Transparency for ratings, fees, and algorithmic decisions
  • Mandated rest periods and dispute resolution access
  • Limits on unpaid waiting time and clearer safety standards

“For too long, digital labour was disguised as ‘entrepreneurship’,” said ADGWU President Anna Liu. “This Act makes clear that algorithmic control is still control — and that means rights.”

If you’re unsure how the Act affects your work, contact us for a member briefing.
